Reported Speech (reporting statements)

Cambia el tiempo verbal de acuerdo a estas tablas.
Direct speech Reported speech
Present simple Past simple
Present continuous Past continuous
am/is/are going to was/were going to
Past simple Past perfect simple
Past continuous Past perfect continuous
Present perfect simple Past perfect simple
Present perfect continuous Past perfect continuous
Past perfect simple Past perfect simple (sin cambio)
Verbos modales
Direct speech Reported speech
will would
can could
must had to
shall should
may might/Could
would would (no cambia)
could could (no cambia)
Should Should (no cambia)
might might (no cambia)
ought to ought to (no cambia)
Expresiones de tiempo, lugar y demostrativos
Direct speech Reported speech
now then
last week the week before
yesterday the previous day/the day before
tonight that night
tomorrow the next day/the following day
today that day
here there
this (book) that (book)
these (books) those (books)

Recuerda: los pronombres personales, adjetivos posesivos y expresiones de tiempo tambien cambian .

Ejercicios

1. Escribe las siguientes frases en "Reported Speech".

  1. My dad: "I don't like computers." (present simple)

    2. My dad said that he didn't like computers. (past simple)

  2. Lorena: "The Kibis are delicious." (present simple)

    3. Lorena said that ____________________________________________. (past simple)

  3. The teacher: "We are going home early." (present continuous)

    4. The teacher said that ____________________________________________. (past continuous)

  4. Miku: "I was very happy." (past simple)

    5. Miku said that ____________________________________________. (past perfect simple)

  5. Alice: "My sister isn't 19 years old." (present simple)

    6. Alice said that ____________________________________________. (past simple)

  6. Tom: "I didn't understand the homework." (past simple)

    7. Tom said that ____________________________________________. (past perfect simple)

  7. Alex: "My dad doesn't do exercise." (present simple)

    8. Alex said that ____________________________________________. (past simple)

2. Escribe las frases en "Direct Speech".

  1. Daniel said that he was studying English at Vida Ingles. (past continuous)

    2. Daniel, "____________________________________________." (present continuous)

  2. Pedro said that he was 12 years old. (past simple)

    3. Pedro, "____________________________________________." (present simple)

  3. My brother said that he wanted to stay up longer. (past simple)

    4. My brother, "____________________________________________." (present simple)

  4. Lupe said that she didn't enjoy the show. (past simple)

    5. Lupe, " ____________________________________________." (present simple)

  5. My uncle said that he hadn't seen the movie. (past perfect simple)

    6. My uncle, "____________________________________________." (past simple)

  8. My neighbour: "I am going to buy a new phone."

    9. My neighbour said that ____________________________________________.

  9. My parents: "We have been coming to this restaurant since 2003."
